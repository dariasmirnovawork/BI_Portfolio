## Personal Finance Report

### Data Source
Data is provided by the private client. Expenses from January to August 2023 year.

### Objectives
The main objectives are:
* to understand which categories have the highest expenditures
* to see clear how the actual spending correlates with the budget
* to explore what trend is observed across different categories

### Deliverables

**1. Deviation from Plan (Budget) analysis:**
  - Overall deviation from Plan (Budget) is currently at 11.48%, and Deviation from Plan for Category Bank Fees is significantly higher than other segments at 116%.

**2. Expenses for Fact goal analysis:**
  - Overall Expenses (40,839) for Fact is above target (36,633). Notably, Category Education (19,394) and Category Paper Work (3,343) are significantly above their individual targets of 17,805 and 2,055, respectively.

**3. Expenses Entertainment goal analysis:**
  - Overall Expenses Entertainment Fact (3,072) is above target ((2,995). Notably, Category Clothes (1,151) is significantly above its individual target of 750.
